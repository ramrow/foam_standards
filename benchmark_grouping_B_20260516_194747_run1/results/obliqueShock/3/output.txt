The Courant number calculation likely involves the acoustic speed, and with the given supersonic inflow (Mach ~3.5 / sqrt(gamma*R/T) ≈ 3.5 / sqrt(1.4*0.7143*1) ≈ 2.8), the initial transient adjustment phase creates huge oscillations.

### New Approach: Add under-relaxation in `fvSolution`

Since `adjustTimeStep yes` alone isn't preventing the crash, we need additional numerical damping to stabilize the simulation from the very first time step. The solution is to add **under-relaxation** for the density (`rho`), momentum (`rhoU`), and energy (`rhoE`) fields in `system/fvSolution`:

```

{
    "(rho|rhoU|rhoE)"
    {
        solver          diagonal;
        relax           0.7;

    U

        solver          smoothSolver;
        smoother        GaussSeidel;
        nSweeps         2;
```
