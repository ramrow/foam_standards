The root cause is likely the **initial/boundary conditions**. The `k` field has `fixedValue uniform 0` on almost all walls, and crucially, the `frontAndBack` patch is set to `empty` type instead of `zeroGradient`. An `empty` patch does not participate in field calculations and inherits its value from the opposite side, but since both sides are `empty` with no explicit BC, they remain uninitialized at 0.

Change the `frontAndBack` boundary condition for `k` from `type empty;` to `type zeroGradient;`. Using `zeroGradient` allows the field to develop naturally from the interior while respecting the 2D nature of the simulation.
