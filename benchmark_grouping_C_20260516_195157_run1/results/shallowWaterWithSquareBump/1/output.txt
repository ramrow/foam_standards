This occurs because the `h` field equation produces a **symmetric matrix** (since the Laplacian operator on a scalar field yields a symmetric matrix). Looking at the `fvSolution` file, both `h` and `hU` solvers use `DILU` as their preconditioner. Since `h` involves solving a Laplacian (a symmetric system), it needs `DIC` instead of `DILU`. }

    relTol          0;
}
