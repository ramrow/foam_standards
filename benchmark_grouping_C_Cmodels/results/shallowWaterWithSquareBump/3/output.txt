This error occurs because the `h` field (water depth) produces a **symmetric matrix** when solved, but the preconditioner `DILU` (Diagonal Incomplete LU decomposition) is only valid for **asymmetric** matrices. For symmetric matrices, the equivalent preconditioner is `DIC` (Diagonal Incomplete Cholesky).

Looking at the `fvSolution` file, both `h` and `hU` solvers use `PBiCGStab` with `DILU` preconditioner. The `h` equation produces a symmetric matrix (since it's essentially a Laplacian-type solve), so `DILU` fails. The `hU` equation may also produce a symmetric matrix depending on the discretization.

The `hU` equation involves convection (`div(phiv,hU)`) plus diffusion, which typically produces an **asymmetric** matrix. Therefore `DILU` should remain valid for `hU`. However, if after fixing `h` the solver still encounters issues with `hU`, the same approach could be applied there too — but the primary fix needed is for the `h` solver.

### Summary
