# Error Analysis and Resolution

## Error 1: `log.blockMesh` — Inward-pointing faces in Block 0

The primary error is that **Block 0** (the inlet straight section) has **inward-pointing faces**. Looking at the current block definition:

```
hex (0 4 5 1 7 6 2 3) (10 10 10) simpleGrading (1 1 1)
```

The vertex ordering `(0 4 5 1 7 6 2 3)` creates a hex block where the **top face** `(4 5 2 6)` and **bottom face** `(0 4 6 7)` are oriented inward. This is the same fundamental issue as in Attempts 1 and 2, but with a different (still incorrect) ordering.

### Root Cause

The vertex ordering for Block 0 is still incorrect. The vertices are listed in a way that creates a "twisted" hexahedron. The correct ordering should ensure all face normals point outward.

### Proposed Solution

**Fix the vertex ordering in Block 0** in `blockMeshDict`. The vertices should be reordered so that all faces have outward-pointing normals. The correct ordering for this block is:

```
hex (0 1 4 5 3 2 7 6) (10 10 10) simpleGrading (1 1 1)
```

This is the standard "bottom-left-front, top-left-front, bottom-right-front, top-right-front, bottom-left-back, top-left-back, bottom-right-back, top-right-back" ordering. This ensures that:
- Face `(0 1 2 3)` (inlet) has outward normal in the -x direction
- Face `(4 5 6 7)` (mid-plane) has outward normal in the +y direction
- Face `(1 4 7 2)` (right wall) has outward normal in the +z direction
- And all other faces similarly have outward-pointing normals.

The relaxation factor for `rho` is set to `0.01`, which is very low and may cause slow convergence or divergence. Consider increasing it to `0.1` or `0.2` for better stability.

hex (0 4 5 1 7 6 2 3) (10 10 10) simpleGrading (1 1 1)

### Root Cause

The vertex ordering for Block 0 is still incorrect. The vertices are listed in a way that creates a "twisted" hexahedron. The correct ordering should ensure all face normals point outward.

```
hex (0 1 4 5 3 2 7 6) (10 10 10) simpleGrading (1 1 1)
```

This is the standard "bottom-left-front, top-left-front, bottom-right-front, top-right-front, bottom-left-back, top-left-back, bottom-right-back, top-right-back" ordering. This ensures that:
- Face `(0 1 2 3)` (inlet) has outward normal in the -x direction
- Face `(4 5 6 7)` (mid-plane) has outward normal in the +y direction
