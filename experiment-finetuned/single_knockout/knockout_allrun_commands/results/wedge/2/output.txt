# Error Analysis and Resolution

## Error 1: `log.blockMesh` — Topology Conflict

The primary error is in `blockMesh`. The mesh definition has a **topology conflict** where the `frontAndBack` patch is incorrectly defined. The issue is that the face `(4 5 6 7)` (the bottom face) is being assigned to both the `bottom` patch (as `symmetryPlane`) and the `frontAndBack` patch (as `empty`). This causes a face to belong to two patches simultaneously, which is invalid.

### Root Cause

In `blockMeshDict`, the `bottom` patch is defined as `symmetryPlane` with face `(4 5 6 7)`, and the `frontAndBack` patch also includes face `(4 5 6 7)`. Since the bottom face is shared by both patches, the mesh topology fails.

### Proposed Solution

**Remove the `bottom` patch from `blockMeshDict`** and instead define the bottom face as part of the `frontAndBack` patch. The `frontAndBack` patch should include both the front face `(0 3 2 1)` and the bottom face `(4 5 6 7)`. The `bottom` patch should be removed entirely since the symmetry plane condition is handled by the `frontAndBack` empty patch.
